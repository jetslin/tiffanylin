Work Term Recap

For my first co-op work term, I worked at ADLINK Technology Inc in Taipei, Taiwan where I worked in a team of 4 to develop the user interface and back-end Python and C++ modules for an edga data analytics software to detect physical devices, while processing and calculating analog input/output and digital input/output. I'm really excited to bring my knowledge and skills to new workplaces! For my second work term, I worked at a startup company called AutoMetrics Manufacturing Technologies Inc. I focused on researching about machine learning models that can be used in the company's main product, Inspection 4.0. I had a rare opportunity to develop and integrate an anomaly detection model in the software with a months worth of thorough research and testing. I also gained the experience of training a model for semantic segmentation. All of this was a great learning opportunity!

For the remainder of my work terms, I worked at Huawei, where I learned a great deal and formed meaningful connections with my colleagues. During my first 8 months, I collaborated with multiple teams—starting with developing an ArkTS auditor, then moving on to audio and signal processing, then finally joining a team focused on computer vision. With this team, we successfully developed and launched the 'AI Best Expression' feature on Huawei Nova 13. Through this project, I gained valuable experience across the entire product development process—from the initial research and slow prototyping stages to the fast-paced delivery phase where we held regular meetings with our partner team at headquarters in China. When I returned to Huawei for my final 8 month co-op term, I rejoined the same time to work on a new deliverable involving SOTA image generation models like Stable Diffusion XL. Along the way, I deepened my understanding of related concepts including LoRAs, ControlNets, and reinforcement learning, even integrating a reward-based model intro our pipeline. In addition to our main project, our team explored potential research opportunities, giving me firsthand experience with the research process. I thoroughly enjoyed my time at Huawei and truly value the technical and professional growth I achieved through these experiences.
